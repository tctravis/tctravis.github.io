About

I am a linocut printmaker lucky to be living in Kendal, close to the mountains of the Lake District and the dales of Yorkshire.

The landscapes and habitats of England provide a constant source of inspiration, from the northern wilds of Cumbria and Derbyshire, to the ancient beech and hornbeam woods of the south-east. I am also drawn to depict the plants, tools and buildings of the English garden.

In all the prints I am attempting to capture a spirit of place within a simple composition. Whether in the domestic garden or wider landscape, I am intrigued by the relationship between natural forms and man-made objects and structures.
