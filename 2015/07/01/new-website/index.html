About

I am a linocut printmaker lucky to be living in Kendal, close to the mountains of the Lake District and the dales of Yorkshire.

The landscapes and habitats of England provide a rich source of inspiration for my prints, from the northern wilds of Cumbria and Derbyshire, to the ancient beech and hornbeam woods of the south-east. I am also drawn to depict the plants, tools and buildings of domestic gardens.

In all my prints I attempt to capture a spirit of place within a simple composition. Whether in the domestic garden or wider landscape, I am often intrigued by the visual relationships between natural forms and man-made objects and buildings.
