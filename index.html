About

I am a printmaker excited to be exploring a new medium. Attempting to capture spirit of place. Particularity of natural form - identify specific tree or plant - portrait. Economy and strength of line. Tradition of print making. Popular, reproductions. Aspire to simplicity and economy of best folk songs. Taken up to look closely at natural and manmade forms. Capture truth of subject in simple, perhaps crude, lines.

I like the simplicity of Jekyll.
